Features for Qiqqa Android App

I have just switched from Mendeley to Qiqqa and wanted to make some suggestions for future releases of the Android app. I think that right now the technological landscape is changing dramatically as many people begin to use their tablets to read, write and work. This makes a feature rich Android app a must-have, especially regarding other players in the market like e.g. mendeley.

Therefore my suggested features for upcoming releases are:

- highlight function (but please not as overlay on top of the text like right now in the PC version)
- drawing function
- support for Samsung S pen (a digitizer pen for cell phones and tablets that is increasingly popular) and palm rejection
- tool bar (symbol for drawing, annotations, etc.)
- scrolling bar with minipreview of pages (pages can be selected by tap)
- zoom to full screen (upon double tap?)
- light version of the app for free and premium version for $1 (more resources to boost further development of the app?)
...

One of my main reasons for switching from Mendeley to Qiqqa was the response to user feedback. Lots of proposed features actually get implemented into the program (moreover within a short amount of time) so that the users really get what they need. This is exceptional!

Keep up the good work! Science depends on you ;)!
