    David Baldwin over 5 years ago
    When you are in a library, look at the top of your screen. The 7th (of 9) icons says "Find Duplicates" -- it is the one between "Find References" and "Export Library".

    Click on that icon, and it begins to look for duplicate files. When it's done, it either says "No duplicates found" (or somethng lke that) in the middle of the screen, or it shows you the duplicate pairs (or whatever). If there are duplicates, then you can decide which one you want to keep and delete the other (right click, then select Delete).

    When you first begin to use Qiqqa, it is in "Novice Mode" where not all features are available.

    To switch to "Expert Mode", click on the "Expert Mode" icon on the "Home" tab. The missing icons (including "Find Duplicates") will appear when you restart Qiqqa.
