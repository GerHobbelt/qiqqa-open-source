    Qiqqa (Official Rep) over 5 years ago
    Qiqqa's watch mechanism remembers everything it ever imported (even items you have since deleted) so that it doesn';t have to process those again.  If you press the 'reset watching history' button, it will forget everything in the past.

    However, Qiqqa also has a duplicate prevention mechanism - and PDFs you have in your library will not be imported a second time - as long as the PDFs are identical.  If you deleted PDFs in Qiqqa, they will come back if they are in the watch folder after you press 'reset watching history'
