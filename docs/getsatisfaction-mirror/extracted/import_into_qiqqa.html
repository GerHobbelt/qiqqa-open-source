    Karl A over 8 years ago
    With the help of Qiqqa support, I found a decent solution while keeping my Zotero-linked PDFs in their own folders:

    I exported all my Zotero references to Bibtex format (including files). Then I imported them into Qiqqa. That brought in the metadata. Then I delete the folder Zotero exported into (Qiqqa support recommended moving it/renaming it as a more conservative move). At that point I am back to just 2 copies of all my PDFs (better than 3), one copy for Zotero and my general use and one copy for Qiqqa. All my tags and metadata seem to have made it into Qiqqa, and I am happy!
