subscript & superscript

Hi,

sub-script and super-scripts are not interpreted by the bibtex engine.
for instance: a part of a paper title "RbTiOAsO< sub> 4" should be interpreted as --> "RbTiOAsO4" and forwarded to the references in the word document correctly. Similarly "x '' 2 ''" should be processed as "x2 "

Example subscript:
H20 - Water
O2 - Oxygen
CO2 - Carbon Dioxide
Example superscript:
23 = 8
14x

PS: the above is not properly rendered because 'sub' and 'sup' are not enabled for the form neither!
